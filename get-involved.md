Southwest Tech is community-run and community-shaped. There are a few ways to get involved.

## Come to a meetup

The simplest thing — just show up. Check the [events page](/events/) for the next one and RSVP on [Lu.ma](https://luma.com/a5tunjsx) so we know you're coming and can send you venue details.

## Suggest a venue

Know a good pub, café, or space in Dunsborough, Busselton, or Margaret River that would work well for a casual tech gathering? We'd love to hear about it.

## Spread the word

Tell someone who might be interested. Post about it. Help us find the people who didn't know this existed.

## Help organise

If you want to be more hands-on — helping with venues, communications, or just wrangling people on the night — get in touch.

## Contact

Reach out via [email](mailto:hello@southwesttech.au) or find us on the socials once we're up and running.
