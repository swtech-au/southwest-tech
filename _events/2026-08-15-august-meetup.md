Thanks to everyone who's made it along so far — and to those who reached out to say they were keen but couldn't make it. Turnout and interest have kept up, so this is officially becoming a regular thing: Southwest Tech, every ~six weeks or so , for as long as people keep showing up.

Same deal every time: show up, find a seat, talk to people about what you're building, breaking, or thinking about. No agenda, no slides, no pressure. Arrive when you like, leave when you like.

Next one's at Rocky Ridge — we'll make sure we get a space reserved. See the event schedule for details.

Whether you've been to every one or this is your first — you're welcome. And if you know someone who might be into this, bring them along or pass this on.

Feedback, thoughts, and suggestions are always welcome.
