Thanks to everyone who made it along to the first one — and to those who reached out to say they were keen but couldn't make it. It went well, and there seems to be enough interest to keep this going, which is great!

So — we're doing it again. Same deal: show up, find a seat, talk to people about what you're building, breaking, or thinking about. No agenda, no slides, no pressure. Arrive when you like, leave when you like.

We'll be back at Rocky Ridge with a space reserved — fingers crossed the weather plays along again.

Whether you were there last time or this is your first — you're welcome. And if you know someone who might be into this, bring them along or pass this on.

Feedback, thoughts, and suggestions are always welcome.
